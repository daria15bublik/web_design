About US

Beauty Check is an independent platform created to help people make smarter and more honest beauty choices.

Today, beauty brands control how their products are presented online. They remove negative reviews and highlight only positive feedback, making it hard for consumers to judge whether a product is really worth buying.

Beauty Check was created to change that.

We believe in independence, transparency, and trust. We are not influenced by brands, sponsorships, or advertising pressure. Our goal is to help users spend their money more consciously and avoid unnecessary purchases.

With Beauty Check, a user can upload or scan a product — even directly in a store — and instantly see real feedback and an overall rating.

This makes it easier to decide whether a product is worth buying before spending money on it.

Beauty Check is about clarity, confidence, and informed choices.
